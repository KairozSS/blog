## flock
- There is a flock of sheeps in the distance.

## estrange
- My circumstances estranged me from my loved ones.
- That past life of mine is completely estrange from the present.
- The sounds produced by her mouth are completely estranged from this world.

## conformity
- All I ever did was in conformity to laws.
- Conformity made you someone without a pinch of personality.

## exhort
*exhort* is just a synonym of *persuade*.

- I exhorted him to go to sleep earlier.
- She exhorted me to finish my degree.
- And then I exhorted myself to quit my job.
- Exhort yourself to take breaks once in a while.

## hefty
*hefty* is an adjective. It means large.

- A hefty sum of money.
- A hefty cat.
- He gave the door a hefty kick.

## commendation
*commendation* is a noun. It means praise.

- Desire for commendation makes us do ridiculous  actions.
- You desire great commendation for your acts.
- They deserve commendation for their work on the translation.

## mainspring
*mainspring* is a noun and it is the main reason why something happens.

- Indolence is the mainspring why global warming continues to be a problem.
- Hard work and persistence is the mainspring of success.
- Limitations are the mainspring of creativity.
- Desire for praise is one of the mainsprings of comformity.

## sober
*sober* is an adjective. It means serious. You can also use soberly and it could mean the same thing or, with moderation or self-restraint; temperately.

- We have to do this with a sober attitude.
- A sober assesment about the dangers of capitalism.
- She soberly did his job.
- I'm taking this situation soberly.
