When I was writing the previous entry, there was a lot to say. Now I sit here, writing this one and now I realize I don't have much to say.

Hehehe, now what?

I only know the beginning and the objective. The objective is to simply make good videos, grow the channel and have fun doing that... and I think that's all I need to know. After all...

*No plan survives contact with the enemy.*

Once I go out there, the plan will simply crumble when is subject to reality. But as long as I know what's the objetive, I'll know what to do according to the circumstances.

The objective I mentioned before is just the bare surface, it goes a little bit deeper than that.

I want to make good videos, because I want to make something that people find valuable and enjoyable, because that's one thing that brings me joy. That's it. I don't care about leaving my mark behind or be remembered, after all, everything has an end and ends up being forgotten... and I'm fine with that. Also, I don't care about making "art" and explore the nuances and limits of it, I'm not interested on that. I simply want to make videos that people find valuable and enjoyable, because again, that's one thing that brings me joy.

Growing the channel is simply a sign that tells me if I'm creating something that people find valuable and they enjoy. If the channel grows I guess people are looking for the content because of that. If the channel doesn't grow, I think it is the opposite... the videos are not good.

And regarding the part of having fun, everything is pointless if I don't. That entails a lot of things. For instance if I make videos that I don't find valuable myself, I will feel I'm wasting my time and therefore not having fun. The same can be said about making videos about topics I'm not interested or I don't believe. Neglecting myself is a no.

Having fun is also what will keep me creating videos for a long time.

So that's the objective and still a question remains... why?

*Why do all this?*

Well, I mentioned I want to make good videos and when people find them valuable and enjoyable, that brings me joy. Having fun is also another one too. Those are not my only reasons though. There's another one.

If this plan is someday a success... I simply want to help out my family if it is possible. After all, what's the point of doing a magnificent act if you don't help the people close to you?
