This entry is going to be short.

I was thinking that I should put effort when it comes to citing my sources on videos.

Originally, I was going to put a number between brackets in a section of a video that required a source or note. Then I was going to link on the description a pastebin listing the sources with the corresponding number.

But do you know what would be better? To make an entire website with a cool design dedicated to list my sources.

Not only I will be able to list my sources there, I could add any extras there related to a particular video without cluttering the video description on Youtube.

Most people don't check citations or sources, especially on a Youtube video, like probably nobody would visit that website. But I don't care, I simply want to do a good job.
