## estrange
- Those ideas were estranged from reality
- I am completely estranged from that corporate world.
- Their dad made them estrange from their mother.

## flock
- You can use the word flock for a group of sheeps, goats and birds.

## bicker
- They always bicker about pointless subjects.
- That bickering they are having right now is so loud.

## sober
- Buying a house or rent... you have to have a sober view to take that decision.
- We have to asses this situation soberly.
- When it comes to alcohol, she takes a sober approach.

## exhort
- Exhort yourself to not get angry to other people.
- But Mike exhorted Cal to do his homework.
- He exhorted everyone to believe he was not the culprit.

## whet
- That shiny colors whetted my curiosity.
- The smell of steak whetts my apettite.
- So prepare yourself, whet your courage and go ahead.
- You can whet knifes with whet stones.
